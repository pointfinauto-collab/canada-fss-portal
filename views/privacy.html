🇨🇦 Privacy Policy

Canada Development Opportunities Portal · Global Affairs Canada

Last updated: June 2026

1. Collection of Personal Information

The Canada Development Opportunities Portal collects personal information including full name, date of birth, nationality, passport details, email address, phone number, country of residence, and professional credentials for the purpose of administering the Field Support Services (FSS) Program.

2. Use of Personal Information

Personal information is used to: verify applicant eligibility, process applications, communicate application status, administer program activities, and fulfill legal obligations under the Privacy Act and the Access to Information Act of Canada.

3. Security of Information

All personal information is protected using industry-standard 256-bit SSL/TLS encryption. Passwords are hashed using BCrypt. Access is restricted to authorized Government of Canada personnel only.

4. Retention of Records

Application records are retained for a period of seven (7) years in accordance with the Government of Canada information management policies.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information by contacting fss-portal@canada.ca.
