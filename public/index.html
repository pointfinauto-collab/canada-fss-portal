Program Overview

The Field Support Services (FSS) Program is a flagship initiative by Global Affairs Canada designed to strengthen institutional capacity, promote good governance, and advance sustainable development across East Africa and the broader Pan-African region.

Through competitive registration, qualified individuals, organizations, and firms are invited to participate in Canada-funded projects that transform communities and build lasting partnerships.

Frequently asked questions
What is the Field Support Services (FSS) Program?⌄
The FSS Program is a Government of Canada initiative managed by Global Affairs Canada. It establishes a roster of pre-qualified individuals, organizations, and firms to deliver technical assistance in Canadian-funded development projects across East Africa.
What is a UIC Number and what is it used for?⌄
A Unique Identification Code (UIC) is automatically generated upon successful registration in the format UIC-CA-2026-XXXXXX. It identifies your application and must be quoted in all official correspondence.
How long does the review process take?⌄
Once all required documents are submitted and fees are paid, reviews typically take 15–30 business days. Complex applications may require additional information.
What documents are required for registration?⌄
Valid passport copy, updated CV/Resume, relevant academic certificates. Organizations must also provide: company registration certificate, tax compliance certificate, and audited financial statements.
Is there an application fee?⌄
Application processing fees are assigned individually based on applicant category (Individual: CAD 150, NGO: CAD 300, Firm/Contractor: CAD 400–500). Fees appear in your dashboard after document review. Payment is accepted via Flutterwave (card, mobile money, bank transfer).
Which payment methods are accepted?⌄
Payments are processed through Flutterwave, supporting: Visa/Mastercard, M-Pesa (Kenya), Telebirr (Ethiopia), MTN Mobile Money, Airtel Money, USSD bank transfer, and Barter. All major African and international cards accepted.
